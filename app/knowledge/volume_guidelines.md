# Quivrr Volume Guidance

Volume is not a perfect measurement, but it is useful for first pass board guidance.

General starting ranges:
- Beginner: about 0.55 to 0.75 litres per kg of body weight.
- Early intermediate: about 0.45 to 0.55 litres per kg.
- Intermediate: about 0.37 to 0.48 litres per kg.
- Advanced: about 0.30 to 0.40 litres per kg.
- Expert: about 0.27 to 0.35 litres per kg.

Adjust volume upward when:
- the surfer wants more paddle power
- waves are soft, weak or small
- the surfer is older, heavier or less surf fit
- the surfer struggles to catch waves
- the surfer is moving down from a bigger board

Adjust volume downward when:
- the surfer is advanced
- waves are powerful or clean
- the surfer already catches waves easily
- the surfer wants tighter, sharper performance

Never rely on volume alone. Outline, rocker, width, rails and construction matter.
